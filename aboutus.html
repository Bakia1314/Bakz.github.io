Bakia Tamoaieta

Managing Director, & Customer Care

Welcome to Bakz & Fam seafood restaurant, where we pride ourselves on serving the freshest and most delicious seafood in town. Our menu offers a wide range of seafood options that will satisfy even the most discerning palate. Whether you're a fan of shrimp, lobster, crab, or fish, we have something for you. We start every day by sourcing the freshest seafood from local fishermen and Divers. Our chefs then prepare each dish to order, ensuring that you get the best quality and taste in every bite. Our seafood is always cooked to perfection, and we have a variety of preparation styles to suit your taste buds. Our signature dishes include succulent shrimp scampi, rich and creamy lobster bisque, and our famous seafood platter that is piled high with the catch of the day. We also offer a variety of vegetarian and gluten-free options, so there is something for everyone. In addition to our delicious food, we also have a well-stocked bar that serves a variety of beers, wines, and cocktails. Our friendly bartenders are always on hand to mix you up a refreshing local drink to complement your meal. Our restaurant has a warm and welcoming atmosphere that is perfect for a romantic dinner for two, a family celebration, or a night out with friends. We have both indoor and outdoor seating, so you can enjoy your meal in the setting of your choice. So, come on down to Bakz & Fam seafood restaurant and experience the best that the ocean has to offer. We can't wait to welcome you and treat you to a meal that you'll never forget.
